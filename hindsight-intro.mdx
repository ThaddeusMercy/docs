# What is Hindsight?  
  
Hindsight VIP is a blockchain analytics and data visualization platform that helps you explore and understand on-chain activity.  
  
## Key features  
  
- Visualize data with shapes and colors for wallets, contracts, and transactions.  
- Simple interface with clear controls.  
- Personalize the display to focus on the information you need.  
- Supports multiple networks like Ethereum, Solana, XRPL and Arbitrum with plans to add Bitcoin.  
  
## Use cases  
  
- Due diligence, audits and fraud detection by spotting risky contracts and wallets.  
- Research and education to help newcomers learn blockchain concepts.  
- Transaction verification and DeFi analytics for real-time insights.  
- Tracking airdrops, tokens and NFTs across chains.  
- Building and testing smart contracts with debugging and simulation tools.
