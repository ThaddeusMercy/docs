# Everything to Know About Hindsight's Samaritan Update  
![Samaritan article header]({{file:942735246996114}})  
The Samaritan update lets you report suspicious wallets, contracts and tokens. Hindsight's team reviews these reports and labels them as scam or malicious so everyone can see the warning.  
## Why it matters  
- Blockchain scams and hacks are common. Community reporting adds an extra layer of safety.  
- You can act as a watchdog by flagging wallets or contracts that seem shady.  
- Once verified, red markers appear on the visual explorer to warn other users.  
## How to report  
- When you spot a suspicious transaction or token, use the report tool to submit details.  
- The team checks the evidence and updates the label if the item is confirmed to be harmful.  
- You will see a red icon next to flagged items in the explorer.  
## Building a safer blockchain  
- More eyes mean fewer scams. Community vigilance keeps the network clean.  
- Transparent labeling builds trust across traders, artists and builders.  
- Join the initiative and help make blockchain safer for everyone.
