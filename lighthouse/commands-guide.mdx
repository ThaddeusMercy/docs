# Commands Guide  

Use these commands in the Lighthouse Helper Bot on Discord. Each command responds with helpful information or performs a tracking action.  

- **/help**: Shows all commands and their descriptions.  
- **/manage Hindsight subscription**: Displays your current Hindsight VIP subscription details.  
- **/watchlist**: Lists and manages your alerts for wallets, tokens, NFTs and spotlights.  
- **/statistics**: Shows your personal tracking stats and usage.  
- **/track wallet**: Tracks activity on a wallet on Ethereum, Solana or XRPL.  
- **/spotlight**: Sends a notification when a wallet sends or receives a specified amount.  
- **/total value**: Checks the total value of assets in a wallet, including staked assets.  
- **/track NFT collection**: Monitors an NFT collection for floor price, listings or sales.  
- **/track token**: Alerts you when a tokenâ€™s price moves significantly.  
- **/token exchange search**: Finds exchanges where a token is traded.  
- **/price search**: Returns the current price for a cryptocurrency or FX pair.
